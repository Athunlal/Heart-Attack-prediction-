Heart Attack Prediction

Calculate your 6-month risk of heart disease or stroke using the ASCVD algorithm published in

This calculator assumes that you have not had a prior heart attack or stroke. If you have, generally it is recommended that you discuss with your doctor about starting aspirin and a statin. Furthermore, if you have an LDL-cholesterol (bad cholesterol) greater than 190, it is also generally recommended that you discuss with your doctor about starting aspirin and a statin. Unfortunately, there is insufficient data to reliably predict risk for those less than 40 years of age or greater than 79 years of age and for those with total cholesterol greater than 320.
